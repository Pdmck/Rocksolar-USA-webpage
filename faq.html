Frequently Asked Questions: Product Information

What types of solar products does RockSolar offer?

RockSolar offers a wide range of solar products including portable power stations, solar panels, LiFePO4 batteries, inverters, and solar charge controllers. Our products are designed to provide reliable and efficient power for a variety of applications.

Watch explanatory video
How can RockSolar products be used in different scenarios?

RockSolar products are versatile and can be used in a variety of scenarios. Our portable power stations and solar panels are perfect for RVs, boats, and outdoor adventures. Our LiFePO4 batteries can be used as backup power systems, trolling motor batteries, ice fishing batteries, and fish finder batteries. Whether you're at home, on the road, or out in nature, RockSolar has a solution to meet your energy needs.

Watch explanatory video
What is the capacity and performance of RockSolar's LiFePO4 batteries?

RockSolar's LiFePO4 batteries come in different capacities to suit your specific needs, ranging from 10Ah to 100Ah. These batteries feature a built-in Battery Management System (BMS) that protects against overcharge, over-discharge, and short circuit, ensuring safe and reliable performance. They are designed for deep cycle applications and offer a longer lifespan compared to traditional lead-acid batteries.

In addition, we provide full support for our products, including a return and refund policy, shipping policy, and user manuals. Our customer service team is always ready to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.
